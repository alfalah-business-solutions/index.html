ABOUT US

Who We Are

Al Falah Business Solutions & General Trading LLC is a UAE-based business consultancy and accounting firm registered in the UAE and classified as a Designated Non-Financial Business and Profession (DNFBP) under UAE AML/CFT regulations.

Our multidisciplinary team of qualified accountants, tax advisors, HR specialists and business consultants delivers customized, compliant and cost-effective financial and business solutions to individuals, startups, SMEs and established enterprises across the UAE.

We serve clients across mainland UAE, free zones and offshore — providing end-to-end support from company formation through to ongoing compliance and growth advisory.
